Fish & Chips Trivia

When did Fish & Chips first appear in the United Kingdom?

The origins of fish and chips is not entirely clear. Fried fish was first introduced and sold by East End Jews, while chips first took off in Lancashire and Yorkshire. But we may never know who was the first to bring the magical combination together. (Source: Royal Museums Greenwich)
